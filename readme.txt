== Description ==

Social Media Smart Share help to you shares your old posts automatically in your social media profile and social groups.

The plugin helps in promoting your website content and Increased Brand Awareness in social media networks.

You can create your schedule according to your choice where you can decide which category and what content you want to share. Also, We are using bitly for URL Shorteners.

<a href="http://www.thedotstore.com/docs/plugins/smart-share-wordpress-plugins/" target="_blank">Help Document</a>

= HOW IT WORKS? =

Step 1: Setup your Social Media Account (eg. Twitter, Facebook, Linkedin) with required detail.
Step 2: Schedule Post as per your choice
Step 3: Automatically start sharing a post on your social media Page or Groups

= Key Features =

* Automatically share your new and old posts on social media like Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn.
* Select multiple categories of a website to share content on social media profile.
* Select the minimum age of posts available for sharing. Like All Time, Last week, Last month, Last Quarter, Last year.
* Choose the number of posts you wants to share social media per day.
* Automatically share Post Title or  " Post Excerpt " on social media.
* Post Category or Post Tags covert into hashtags and shares on the social media.
* Set image priority like Features Image, Image from Content, Customer Field.
* Include links back to your site.
* Compatible with URL Shorteners.

= INSTALL INSTRUCTIONS =
This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

* Upload the folder "social-media-smart-share" folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory of your site.
* Activate the plugin through the ‘Plugins’ menu in wordpress.
